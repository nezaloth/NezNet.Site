About Me

I am Nezaloth or Nez, I go by she/her and are transgender and bisexual. My interests include zoology, geology, programming and CGI.

I currently live in New Zealand and had emigrated from the United Kingdom following the 2008 financial crisis. I am very grateful for this, as it has allowed me to escape the current political hellscape in britain and made it possible for me to obtain hormone therapy and medically transition.

At the time of writing I am three months into gender affirming hormone therapy, taking estradiol valerate and spironolactone. In the future I plan on writing the occasional blog post about my progress and experiance with hormones, as it may be of interest for any transfems considering HRT.
