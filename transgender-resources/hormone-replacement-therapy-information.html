Hormone Replacement Therapy Information

Hormone replacement therapy (HRT), sometimes more formally referred to as gender affirming hormone therapy (GAHT), is a range of medications that alter the levels of different hormones within the body's endocrine system [1]. This slowly alters the body over time, making it more masculine/feminine [1].

Among transgender people it has been shown the effects of GAHT can help minimize gender dysphoria and significantly improve quality of life [1]. This makes it a common choice along transition timelines [2].

Broadly, HRT can be categorized into three main treatments, masculinizing [2], feminizing [3] and puberty blockers [4].
